FAQs

How early should I book an event yacht in Goa?

For weekends and sunset slots, booking earlier is recommended. For weekday slots, availability is usually better. Share your date and guest count and we’ll confirm the best options quickly.

Can I customise décor, music, and photography?

Yes—most events can include decorations, photography, videography, and drone shots as add-ons, depending on timing and yacht policy.

What should guests carry?

Carry an ID, comfortable footwear, and light layers for evening cruises. Avoid bringing valuables. Follow crew safety instructions during boarding and cruising.

Is it safe for families and kids?

Safety is a priority—life-saving equipment is available onboard and crew will guide you. For kids, supervision by guardians is recommended at all times.
