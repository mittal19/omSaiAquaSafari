About Azimut 45

The Azimut 45 is a masterpiece of Italian design and engineering excellence, offering an unmatched experience on the water. This luxurious yacht combines elegant styling, exceptional comfort, and outstanding performance.

With its sleek profile and spacious interiors, the Azimut 45 provides the perfect setting for day trips, weekend getaways, or extended cruising adventures. The yacht features three beautifully appointed cabins, including a full-beam master suite, a VIP cabin forward, and a twin cabin, comfortably accommodating up to 6 guests overnight.

The main deck offers a generous salon with panoramic windows, flooding the space with natural light and providing breathtaking views. The exterior spaces include a large cockpit perfect for al fresco dining and a spacious flybridge equipped with sunpads, seating areas, and a wet bar.

Powered by twin Volvo Penta IPS 600 engines, the Azimut 45 delivers impressive performance with a top speed of 31 knots and a cruising speed of 25 knots, ensuring an exhilarating yet comfortable ride.
